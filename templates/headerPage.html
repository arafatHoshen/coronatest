Why Try COVID Predictor?

Early diagnosis is crucial to prevent the development of a disease that may cause danger to human lives. This web application can be used to predict the presence of COVID-19 using the COVID-19 Symptoms if there is no available COVID-19 testing kits, or while waiting for PCR or Swab Test Results.
